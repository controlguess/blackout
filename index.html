About Blackout

What is Blackout?

Blackout is a powerful IR Bomber script designed specifically for the M5StickC Plus2 running Bruce firmware. It's essentially a TV-B-Gone on steroids, giving you the ability to control multiple TV brands with a single device.

How Does It Work?

The script leverages the M5StickC Plus2's built-in IR transmitter to send infrared signals that mimic TV remote controls. By cycling through various manufacturer codes, Blackout can interact with most modern televisions.
